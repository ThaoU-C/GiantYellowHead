GIANT YELLOW HEAD

This artwork is created to demonstrate the interaction of vivid colors and strange patterns in an unusual composition. Being unusual is fun and normal!

The original artwork is a paper cutout and composed into a digital collage. The collage is turned into a 3D environment, where the viewer can interact with the work by navigating around the space.
